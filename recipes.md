# 7-Day LDL-Lowering Meal Plan

Goal: lower LDL cholesterol and support HDL improvement over the next 3 months.

Focus:
- High fibre foods: oats, lentils, beans, chickpeas, vegetables, fruit
- Lean protein: chicken, tuna, eggs, lean beef mince, lean lamb
- Healthy fats: avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds
- Lower saturated fat: limit butter, cream, cheese, fatty meat, fried food

---

## Daily Rules

- Eat oats or another high-fibre breakfast most days.
- Add vegetables to lunch and dinner.
- Use olive oil or canola oil instead of butter.
- Choose lean meat and keep portions controlled.
- Include beans, lentils or chickpeas most days.
- Keep beef and lamb to 1–2 meals per week.
- Prefer tuna in spring water.
- Use Greek yoghurt instead of mayo or creamy sauces.

---

# 7-Day Meal Plan

## Day 1

### Breakfast: Oats, Chia and Yoghurt Bowl

#### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 3/4 cup low-fat Greek yoghurt
- 1/2 cup berries or chopped apple
- Cinnamon
- 1 tbsp chopped almonds or walnuts

#### Method
1. Add oats, chia seeds, yoghurt and fruit to a bowl.
2. Mix well.
3. Add cinnamon.
4. Top with chopped nuts.
5. Eat immediately or leave overnight in the fridge.

---

### Lunch: Chicken Avocado Salad Wrap

#### Ingredients
- 1 wholemeal wrap
- 120–150g cooked chicken breast
- 1/4 avocado
- Lettuce or spinach
- Tomato
- Cucumber
- 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Chilli flakes

#### Method
1. Slice or shred the cooked chicken.
2. Mix chicken with Greek yoghurt, lemon juice, pepper and chilli flakes.
3. Add lettuce, tomato, cucumber and avocado to the wrap.
4. Add chicken mixture.
5. Wrap and serve.

---

### Dinner: Chicken Lentil Curry

#### Ingredients
- 150g chicken breast or skinless chicken thigh
- 1/2 cup cooked lentils or 1/4 cup dry red lentils
- 1 onion
- 2 tomatoes or 1/2 can crushed tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves
- Small piece of ginger
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- Chilli powder to taste
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1–2 cups spinach
- 1 tsp olive oil or canola oil
- 1/2 cup cooked brown rice

#### Method
1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Cook onion, garlic and ginger for 2–3 minutes.
3. Add turmeric, cumin, chilli and garam masala.
4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
5. Add chicken and lentils.
6. Add a little water and simmer until chicken is cooked.
7. Stir in spinach at the end.
8. Serve with brown rice.

---

### Snack
- 1 apple
- Small handful of almonds

---

## Day 2

### Breakfast: Egg White Veggie Omelette

#### Ingredients
- 1 whole egg
- 2–3 egg whites
- Spinach
- Mushroom
- Tomato
- Onion
- 1 slice wholegrain toast
- 1/4 avocado
- Pepper

#### Method
1. Cook onion, mushroom, tomato and spinach in a non-stick pan.
2. Beat egg and egg whites together.
3. Pour eggs over the vegetables.
4. Cook until set.
5. Serve with wholegrain toast and avocado.

---

### Lunch: Tuna Chickpea Bowl

#### Ingredients
- 1 can tuna in spring water, drained
- 1/2 can chickpeas, rinsed
- Cucumber
- Tomato
- Red onion
- Spinach
- Lemon juice
- 1 tsp olive oil
- Pepper
- Chilli flakes
- Optional: 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt

#### Method
1. Add tuna, chickpeas and vegetables to a bowl.
2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, pepper and chilli.
3. Mix well.
4. Add Greek yoghurt if you want it creamier.

---

### Dinner: Lean Beef Mince Chilli

#### Ingredients
- 150g lean beef mince
- 1/2 can kidney beans or black beans
- 1/2 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 capsicum
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Chilli powder to taste
- 1/2 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa

#### Method
1. Brown the beef mince in a pan.
2. Drain excess fat if needed.
3. Add onion, garlic and capsicum.
4. Add tomatoes, beans and spices.
5. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
6. Serve with brown rice or quinoa.

---

### Snack
- Carrot sticks
- Cucumber sticks
- Hummus

---

## Day 3

### Breakfast: Overnight Oats with Banana

#### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds or ground flaxseed
- 1/2 banana
- Low-fat milk or unsweetened soy milk
- Cinnamon
- 1 tbsp peanut butter or chopped nuts

#### Method
1. Add oats, chia/flaxseed, banana, milk and cinnamon to a container.
2. Mix well.
3. Refrigerate overnight.
4. Top with peanut butter or nuts before eating.

---

### Lunch: Chicken and Bean Soup

#### Ingredients
- 120g chicken breast
- 1/2 can cannellini beans or lentils
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stick
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- Spinach
- Low-salt stock
- Pepper
- Mixed herbs

#### Method
1. Add onion, garlic, carrot and celery to a pot.
2. Add chicken, beans and stock.
3. Simmer until chicken is cooked.
4. Shred the chicken.
5. Add spinach at the end.
6. Season with pepper and herbs.

---

### Dinner: Tuna Wholemeal Pasta

#### Ingredients
- 1 can tuna in spring water
- 1 cup cooked wholemeal pasta
- 1/2 can crushed tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves
- Spinach
- Broccoli
- Chilli flakes
- 1 tsp olive oil

#### Method
1. Cook pasta.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
3. Add garlic, tomatoes, broccoli and spinach.
4. Add tuna.
5. Mix in cooked pasta.
6. Season with chilli flakes and pepper.

---

### Snack
- Greek yoghurt
- Berries

---

## Day 4

### Breakfast: Savoury Oats with Egg

#### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- Water or low-salt stock
- Spinach
- Mushroom
- 1 whole egg
- Pepper
- Chilli flakes
- Spring onion

#### Method
1. Cook oats with water or low-salt stock.
2. Stir in spinach and mushroom.
3. Cook or poach one egg separately.
4. Top oats with egg.
5. Add pepper, chilli flakes and spring onion.

---

### Lunch: Chicken Quinoa Salad

#### Ingredients
- 120–150g grilled chicken
- 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
- Spinach or lettuce
- Tomato
- Cucumber
- Capsicum
- 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- Lemon juice
- 1 tsp olive oil
- Pepper

#### Method
1. Add quinoa, vegetables and chicken to a bowl.
2. Add lemon juice, olive oil and pepper.
3. Top with pumpkin seeds.
4. Mix and serve.

---

### Dinner: Lamb and Vegetable Bowl

#### Ingredients
- 120–150g lean lamb, trimmed of visible fat
- Kumara or potato
- Broccoli
- Carrot
- Zucchini
- 1 tsp olive oil
- Garlic
- Rosemary
- Pepper

#### Method
1. Roast kumara or potato with vegetables using minimal oil.
2. Season lamb with garlic, rosemary and pepper.
3. Grill or pan-sear lamb.
4. Serve lamb with roasted vegetables.
5. Keep vegetables as the largest part of the plate.

---

### Snack
- Orange or kiwifruit
- Small handful of walnuts

---

## Day 5

### Breakfast: Greek Yoghurt Protein Bowl

#### Ingredients
- 1 cup low-fat Greek yoghurt
- 1/4 cup rolled oats
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Berries or chopped apple
- Cinnamon
- 1 tbsp almonds

#### Method
1. Add yoghurt to a bowl.
2. Mix in oats and chia seeds.
3. Add fruit and cinnamon.
4. Top with almonds.

---

### Lunch: Egg and Tuna Salad Sandwich

#### Ingredients
- 1 can tuna in spring water
- 1 boiled egg
- 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
- Mustard or lemon juice
- Lettuce
- Tomato
- 2 slices wholegrain bread
- Pepper

#### Method
1. Mash tuna, boiled egg, Greek yoghurt and mustard/lemon together.
2. Add pepper.
3. Add lettuce and tomato to bread.
4. Add tuna egg mixture.
5. Serve as a sandwich.

---

### Dinner: Chicken Stir-Fry

#### Ingredients
- 150g chicken breast
- Broccoli
- Capsicum
- Carrot
- Bok choy or cabbage
- Garlic
- Ginger
- 1 tsp olive oil or canola oil
- Low-salt soy sauce
- 1/2 cup cooked brown rice

#### Method
1. Slice chicken and vegetables.
2. Heat oil in a pan.
3. Cook chicken until browned.
4. Add garlic, ginger and vegetables.
5. Add low-salt soy sauce.
6. Serve with brown rice.

---

### Snack

- Wholegrain crackers

---

#### Ingredients
- 1 whole egg

- 1/2 cup low-salt baked beans or black beans
- 1 slice wholegrain toast
- Spinach
- Tomato
- Pepper

#### Method

- Tomato
- Cucumber

- Celery
- Tomato
- Spinach
- Garlic

## Day 7

### Breakfast: Oat Pancakes

#### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 banana
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
- Cinnamon
- Optional: 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
- Berries for topping

#### Method
1. Blend or mash oats, banana, egg, yoghurt and cinnamon.
2. Cook small pancakes in a non-stick pan.
3. Serve with berries and extra yoghurt.

---

### Lunch: Chicken Hummus Bowl

#### Ingredients
- 120–150g cooked chicken
- 2 tbsp hummus
- Lettuce or spinach
- Cucumber
- Tomato
- Carrot
- 1/2 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
- Lemon juice
- Pepper

#### Method
1. Add rice or quinoa to a bowl.
2. Add vegetables and chicken.
3. Add hummus and lemon juice.
4. Season with pepper.

---

### Dinner: Tuna and Bean Patties

#### Ingredients
- 1 can tuna in spring water, drained
- 1/2 can cannellini beans or chickpeas, mashed
- 1 egg
- Spring onion
- Pepper
- Chilli flakes
- Parsley
- 2 tbsp oats or wholemeal breadcrumbs
- Salad vegetables
- Roasted vegetables

#### Method
1. Mash beans in a bowl.
2. Add tuna, egg, spring onion, pepper, chilli and oats/breadcrumbs.
3. Shape into patties.
4. Pan-cook with minimal oil.
5. Serve with salad and roasted vegetables.

---

### Snack
- Greek yoghurt or fruit

# Meal Prep Plan

## Cook Once, Use Multiple Times

### Chicken
Cook 1kg chicken breast at the start of the week.

Use for:
- Chicken avocado wrap
- Chicken quinoa salad
- Chicken hummus bowl
- Chicken stir-fry
- Chicken soup

### Brown Rice or Quinoa
Cook 3–4 cups cooked brown rice or quinoa.

Use for:
- Chicken lentil curry
- Beef chilli
- Chicken stir-fry
- Beef rice bowl
- Chicken hummus bowl

### Eggs
Boil 4–6 eggs.

Use for:
- Tuna egg sandwich
- Snacks
- Breakfast bowls

### Lentils and Beans
Keep cans ready or cook a batch of lentils.

Use for:
- Lentil curry
- Chicken stew
- Soups
- Tuna patties
